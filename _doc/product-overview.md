# Product Overview — RevPath (working name)

> Working name "RevPath" is provisional, pending the branding pass. Update here first
> when the name is locked, then reconcile the blueprint.

## One-liner
An AI strategist that ideates and pressure-tests monetization models for seed-stage,
tech-based companies — turning a founder's late-night guess into a repeatable,
defensible revenue playbook.

## The problem
Seed-stage tech companies rarely die from bad product — they stall because the
monetization model is bolted on too late and copied from whoever raised most recently.
Sharp monetization thinking today lives in expensive, slow, bespoke advisory work:
hours of a consultant's or operator's time per company, hard to scale across many
startups.

## What it is
A focused web app where the user describes a company (stage, sector, product, who
pays, market, current traction) and RevPath generates tailored monetization strategy
options — pricing models, revenue streams, packaging, and go-to-market-aligned
recommendations — each ranked with rationale, key assumptions, and the main risks.
The output is a structured, shareable strategy memo, not a generic chatbot answer.

## Primary user
Someone who works across **multiple** seed-stage tech companies, not just their own:
an accelerator or venture studio operator, an early-stage fund's platform/portfolio
team, or a fractional/advisory practice. Secondary user: a seed-stage founder pressure-
testing their own model. The "various companies" framing in the request points to a
portfolio/advisory context as the lead persona.

## Core workflow (the verified operation)
1. User enters a company profile (stage, sector, product, customer, market, traction).
2. RevPath generates 2–4 monetization strategy options tailored to that profile.
3. Each option is ranked and explained: rationale, pricing/revenue mechanics, key
   assumptions, and primary risks.
4. User saves/exports the strategy as a shareable memo for the founder or team.

**Verified operation:** *a monetization strategy generated for a company* (a completed,
saved strategy — not a click, login, or page view).

## Value
Replaces and accelerates expensive, bespoke advisory work. A bespoke monetization
strategy memo normally takes a skilled operator several hours; RevPath produces a strong
first draft in minutes, letting the advisor refine rather than start from scratch — and
scales that across an entire portfolio.

## Scope (MVP)
- Company-profile intake form.
- AI generation of ranked monetization strategy options with rationale, assumptions,
  and risks.
- Saved strategies library, viewable and exportable per company.
- Out of scope for MVP: live market data integrations, financial modeling/forecasting
  spreadsheets, multi-user collaboration, CRM sync.

## Tone & brand
Sharp, credible, operator-grade. Calm and editorial — built for founders, funds, and
advisors. Not hypey; confident and substance-first.

## Strategic principles
- Substance over volume: a few sharp, defensible options beat a wall of generic ideas.
- Always show the reasoning — rationale, assumptions, and risks, never a bare verdict.
- Tailored to the company's actual stage and market, never one-size-fits-all.
- Built to scale across many companies, so a portfolio operator gets leverage.
